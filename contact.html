What happens next

Jay or Ian reads every enquiry personally. The one whose work it touches replies with the next move: a quick question, a Calendly link, or a short proposal.

How long it usually takes

Within 24 hours on weekdays. Weekend submissions roll into Monday morning. If it’s been longer than 48 hours, your spam folder is the first place to check.

What we may ask you

  • Your business URL (or a sketch of who you serve, if you don’t have one yet)
  • Any deadline that matters
  • The rough budget shape ($1.5k? $15k? $50k?)
  • What you’ve already tried
  • Whether you’re talking to other agencies

What you can prepare

  • Any rough notes or sketches you’ve made already
  • Two or three examples of work you admire (or hate)
  • Names of competitors you don’t want to look like

What not to stress about

You don’t need a polished brief. You don’t need to call. You don’t need to have all the answers. The first reply is a conversation, not a quote.

If it’s genuinely urgent

Contract about to renew, site down, agency ghosting you, something on fire? Email jayden@plainblackcreative.com or ian@plainblackcreative.com directly with “URGENT” in the subject line. That’s faster than waiting on a form reply.

Otherwise

You’ll hear from us. Talk soon.— Jay & Ian
