1
Fastest free hosting: GitHub Pages in 10 minutes
▾

Create a GitHub account and new repository named gromad. Upload gromad-homepage.html and rename it to index.html. Go to Settings → Pages → Source: Deploy from branch → main. Your site will be live at yourusername.github.io/gromad within 2 minutes.

To connect your custom domain: add a CNAME file to the repo containing just gromad.com, then add a CNAME DNS record at your registrar pointing to yourusername.github.io. SSL is automatic.

2
Wiring Web3Forms in 5 minutes
▾

Go to web3forms.com, sign up with your Gmail, and get your access key. In the homepage HTML, find the email form and add: <input type="hidden" name="access_key" value="YOUR_KEY">. Change the form action to https://api.web3forms.com/submit. Done. Every submission hits your Gmail inbox instantly, free forever.

1
Recording a guest interview via Zoom — step by step
▾

In Zoom: Settings → Recording → Enable local recording. Start a meeting with your guest. Hit Record → Record on this computer. Both video tracks record separately. After the call, Zoom exports MP4 files to your Documents/Zoom folder.

Edit in CapCut (free) or DaVinci Resolve (free). Add an intro card with the GR/OMAD amber logo, cut silences, add your episode title as a lower-third. Export at 1080p. Upload to YouTube as unlisted first, check the cut, then publish.

Paid Acquisition — META ads to drive product sales
Pending
▾

META is the right channel to start. The audience is on Facebook and Instagram, they are decision-makers, and the targeting lets you reach people by lifestyle signals and life events. Start at $30/day and don't touch it for 72 hours.

Campaign setup checklist
✓
META Business Manager created and Pixel installed on gromad.com homepage
✓
Pixel event tested: PageView fires on homepage load, Lead fires on form submission
✓
Campaign objective: Sales (not Traffic — you want buyers, not clickers)
✓
Audience: AU + NZ + US, ages 35-65, interests: caravanning, motorhomes, financial independence, early retirement, van life
✓
Budget: $30/day USD. Do not increase for 7 days minimum.
✓
3 ad variations created: 1 video (talking head or rig footage), 1 static (bold headline + tagline), 1 carousel (3 guide benefits)
Ad copy — proven angles to test
1
3 ad copy variants ready to paste
▾

Angle 1 — The math: "Your mortgage costs $2,800/month. Full-time Gromad life costs $1,400. Same life. Half the price. Different address every week. The Gromad Setup Guide shows you exactly how to make the switch in 90 days. $97. No agency. No ongoing fees."

Angle 2 — The fear removal: "Everyone who's done it said the same thing: the fear was worse than the reality. The Gromad Setup Guide eliminates the stuff people get wrong — vehicle choice, legal setup, rego, insurance, connectivity. $97. Everything in order."

Angle 3 — The identity: "You've been talking about it for three years. The rig. The road. The life. At some point talking about it stops and doing it starts. The Gromad Setup Guide is for when you're ready to start."

⚠
Common mistake: Increasing budget before the Pixel has enough data. META needs 50 purchase events per ad set before its algorithm optimises properly. At $30/day this takes roughly 2 weeks. Increasing budget before this point resets the learning phase and wastes spend.
