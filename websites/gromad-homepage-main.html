What is a Gromad?
GROMAD
/groh·mad/ — noun, identity, lifestyle

A person who chose movement over mortgage. Who traded the fixed address for the open road, the quiet spot, the long stay.

Not running from something. Running toward everything. The rig is the home. The community is the family. The world is the suburb.
