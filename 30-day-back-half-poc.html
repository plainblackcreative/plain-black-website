The Premise

Cool builds get applause. Painful, specific, useful builds get invoices.

PlainBlack does not ship calculators. We ship tools we would happily use ourselves, aimed at pains people either grind through quietly or pay way too much to solve badly (usually with a fix that comes with new problems). Objects with personality, live meters, shareable artefacts, and the kind of voice that makes a roofer screenshot it to his mate.

The framework is the table. The voice, the specificity, and the speed of execution are the food. AI gets you the framework. Imagination is what makes the framework spread.

GPT thinks PlainBlack voice is a profanity filter. Add some "fucks" and "cunts," recycle a Reddit joke, ship. That is not the voice. The voice is the context, the big picture, and the result behind it. The difference between a selfie reframed as a questionably-copyrighted Instagram post, and a scalable, tangible piece of delivery that actually turns a profit.

Two good cunts with big hearts, big imaginations, and high standards, plus an army of AI death robots we have repurposed to help us help you do better. Every day a build. Once a week a public audit. One capstone that does not try to sell you anything, because the audience already knows where to find us. The whole thing threads back through the first eleven builds, because nothing in the back half exists in a vacuum.
