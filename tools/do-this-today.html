Why this exists

Most small business owners don’t need more strategy. They need today to stop leaking.

You already know what your business needs in the next 90 days. The problem is the next 90 minutes. You sit down with 50 open tabs in your head, pick the wrong three, and feel busy without moving anything that matters.

This card doesn’t care about your annual plan. It cares about what is hand-on-fire today and what to do about it before lunch. Two versions of the same fix, so you can pick the one that fits the gap in your day.

If the card said something obvious, the obvious thing is probably what you should be doing. If it said something uncomfortable, that’s usually a sign.
