Getting Started

Five minutes and we're in.

To start working on your accounts, we need access to a few platforms. Here's exactly how to add us to each one, step by step. You keep full ownership the whole time.

Add this email address to every platform below
info@plainblackcreative.com
Copy it once, paste it five times. You're done.

Five platforms, one email address.

Work through each section below. If you only use some of these platforms, skip the ones that don't apply. Not sure? Add us and we'll sort out what we actually need once we're in.

Meta Business Manager

So we can manage your Facebook Page, Instagram account, and run ads on your behalf, all from one place.

Role to select: Admin
  1. Go to business.facebook.com and sign in to your account.
  2. Click the Settings gear icon in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar.
  3. In the left menu, under "Users," click People.
  4. Click the blue "Add people" button in the top right.
  5. Enter info@plainblackcreative.com and press Enter.
  6. Set the role to Business admin access (or "Employee" if you prefer, then choose which assets to share on the next screen).
  7. Click Next, tick the pages and ad accounts you want us to manage, then click Invite.

We'll get an email invitation and accept it from our end. You'll see us show up under "People" once we do.

Google Business Profile

So we can update your listing, respond to reviews, add photos, and keep your hours and contact details accurate.

Role to select: Manager
  1. Go to business.google.com and sign in to the Google account that owns your listing.
  2. Click on your business name to open the dashboard.
  3. In the left menu, click Business Profile settings.
  4. Click Managers.
  5. Click the person-with-a-plus icon to add a new manager.
  6. Enter info@plainblackcreative.com.
  7. Set the role to Manager, then click Invite.

If you have multiple locations, repeat steps 4 to 7 for each one.

Google Search Console

So we can see how your website ranks in Google, spot technical issues, and track which search terms bring people to you.

Permission to select: Full
  1. Go to search.google.com/search-console and sign in.
  2. Select your website property from the dropdown in the top-left corner.
  3. In the left menu, scroll to the bottom and click Settings.
  4. Click Users and permissions.
  5. Click Add user (the "+" button in the top right of that section).
  6. Enter info@plainblackcreative.com.
  7. Set permission to Full, then click Add.

No invitation email is sent for Search Console, the access is active immediately once you click Add.

Google Ads

So we can create, manage, and optimise your Google ad campaigns, keywords, and budgets without needing your login.

Link method: Manager account
Our manager account ID 240-967-2782
  1. Go to ads.google.com and sign in to your account.
  2. Click the tools icon (wrench, top right corner) to open the Tools and settings menu.
  3. Under the "Setup" column, click Access and security.
  4. Click the Managers tab at the top of the page.
  5. Click the blue "+" button.
  6. Enter our manager account ID: 240-967-2782
  7. Click Send request.

We'll accept the request from our end and your account will appear in our dashboard. This is cleaner than a user invite because our whole team can access it, not just one person.

Google Analytics

So we can track what's working on your website, where traffic comes from, and how visitors behave once they arrive.

Role to select: Editor
  1. Go to analytics.google.com and sign in.
  2. Click the Admin gear icon in the bottom-left corner.
  3. In the middle "Property" column, click Property access management.
  4. Click the blue "+" button in the top right, then click "Add users".
  5. Enter info@plainblackcreative.com.
  6. Select the role Editor.
  7. Click Add.

If your account has multiple properties (for example, if you run more than one website), repeat these steps for each one you want us to work on.

Stuck somewhere?

Platforms update their interfaces regularly and a step might look different to what's described above. Send us a message and we'll walk you through it, or hop on a quick screen share and we'll do it together.
