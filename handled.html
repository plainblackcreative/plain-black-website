The fine print

This isn't a retainer. It's month-to-month work that has to keep earning its place.

A traditional retainer locks you in and bills you whether the work is useful or not. Handled works the other way. The only thing keeping you here should be the work making sense.

Month to month

No annual tie-in. Every month has to earn the next one.

No lock-in contracts

Plain terms, not a trap. Scope, access and ownership clear before we start.

Cancel anytime

Give the word and we stop at month end. Your accounts and assets stay yours.

Approve every invoice

Every bill is a decision you made. No "where did that come from?" line items.

Most agencies lock you in and coast. We'd rather earn the next month than trap you in the last one.

One seat per lane

We don't work for your competitors.

Handled is selective because it has to be. We can't do this properly for everyone, so we only take one client per category, per region. Category and region are agreed before we start, because "competitor" can get messy fast. When your lane is taken, it's taken, and we won't sell the same thinking to the business across the road. It keeps the work sharper, and it keeps your edge yours.
