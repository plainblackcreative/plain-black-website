How It Works

Simple. Transparent. Effective.

No fundraising events to organise. No products to sell. No cold calls. Just share a link and let the work do the rest.

1

We Create Your Custom Page

We build a dedicated landing page with your club's name, logo, and story. It's fully branded to you, hosted by PlainBlack, and ready to share.

2

You Share It With Your Network

Post the link on your social pages, add it to your email newsletter, put it in your match day program. Anywhere your supporters and community are.

3

We Track It. You Get Paid.

Every visitor from your link is tracked. When someone becomes a PlainBlack client, we donate 10% of the project value directly to your club or cause. No cap. No minimum. Every project counts.

See It in Action

What This Looks Like for a Real Club

Mount Maunganui U16 Hockey Club

A real example of how the program works

The club shares their custom PlainBlack GivesBack link in their weekly email newsletter and Facebook page. Over a season, 12 parents and supporters click through and enquire about PlainBlack services.

Three of them become clients: one books a Brand Sprint for their new business, one gets a Google Reviews Playbook, and one commissions a full website and logo package.

The club receives 10% of each project value, paid directly into their account. No admin, no invoicing, no chasing. Just a transfer and a thank you.

Common Questions

Got Questions?

Every club gets a unique URL with a tracking parameter. When someone visits PlainBlack through your link and submits an enquiry, that lead is tagged to your club. If they become a client, the attribution stays with you. We're transparent about the tracking and you can ask us for a report at any time.
We pay out when a project is completed and the client has paid their invoice. We transfer directly to your club's bank account with a clear description of which project the payment relates to. You'll always know exactly what you earned and why.
No minimum size. We work with junior sports clubs of twenty kids and national-level organisations alike. The only requirement is that you're a genuine club, group, or not-for-profit in Australia or New Zealand. We do a quick check before setting up your page, but the bar is low and the intention is to include as many good causes as possible.
It's a dedicated PlainBlack page featuring your club's name, logo, and a short description of your cause. It explains the GivesBack program, shows PlainBlack's services, and makes it easy for visitors to enquire. The page is hosted by PlainBlack so there's nothing for you to manage technically.
Zero. There are no fees to join, no ongoing costs, and no commitment required. You share the link when you want to. The program is designed to give community organisations a passive fundraising stream with no overhead. If it doesn't work out, there's nothing to cancel.
The tracking cookie stays active for 30 days. If someone clicks your link, goes away, and comes back within a month to enquire, the lead is still attributed to your club. After 30 days, the attribution resets.
