Section 01
Strategy Overview
META Google AI-Driven
Goal: Get Mint Exterior in front of homeowners in Northern Tasmania who are actively planning outdoor improvements — and turn those views into booked quotes with minimal manual effort from Jackson.

📌 The Core Problem

  • Great word-of-mouth but no consistent digital lead pipeline
  • Competitors in TAS running basic ads — low bar to outperform
  • No retargeting of website visitors happening
  • Seasonal demand spikes not being capitalised on

✅ The Strategy

  • Two-channel attack: META for awareness + desire, Google for intent capture
  • Before/after content as the creative engine
  • Automated lead nurture so Jackson doesn't chase quotes
  • AI tools to handle 80% of content creation and reporting
Key principle: Mint's brand is built on trust, craftsmanship, and reliability. Every ad needs to feel like a recommendation from a neighbour — not a pushy sales pitch. Authentic photos of real Tassie backyards beat polished stock imagery every time.

Target Audience & Segmentation

  • Rental property owners improving curb appeal
  • Small developers needing reliable sub-trades
  • Airbnb hosts wanting backyard upgrades
  • Target via "real estate investing" and "property management" interests

🎯 META Custom Audiences to Build

  • Website visitors (past 180 days) — retargeting warmest leads
  • Instagram/Facebook page engagers (past 365 days)
  • Video viewers (watched 50%+ of any video) — interest confirmed
  • Lookalike audiences (1–3%) based on existing customers
  • Email list upload of past quote requests for exclusion + lookalike
🛠️ How to build your custom audiences in META — step by step
▼ Show steps
1
Open Audiences in Meta Ads Manager
Go to business.facebook.com → Ads Manager → top menu → Audiences. If you haven't already, create a free Meta Business account at business.facebook.com/overview.
Open Meta Audiences →
2
Create Website Visitors audience
Click "Create Audience" → Custom Audience → Website. Select "All website visitors" with a 180-day window. Name it "Website Visitors 180d". This requires the Meta Pixel to be installed first (see Section 3 setup steps).
3
Create Page Engagers audience
Click "Create Audience" → Custom Audience → Instagram Account (or Facebook Page). Select "Everyone who engaged with your Page" → 365 days. Name it "Page Engagers 365d".
4
Create Video Viewers audience
Create Audience → Custom Audience → Video. Choose "People who watched at least 50% of your video." Select all your existing project Reels and videos. Set to 365 days. Name it "Video Viewers 50pct".
5
Build a Lookalike Audience
Once your custom audiences have at least 100 people in them (takes a few weeks), go back and click "Create Audience" → Lookalike Audience. Select "Website Visitors 180d" as the source. Set location to Australia, size to 1%. This finds new people who behave like your existing visitors.
Note: Audiences with fewer than 100 people won't be usable for ads yet. Build your organic presence first, let the pixel collect data for 2–4 weeks, then create these audiences.

Section 03
META Ads Campaign Structure
Facebook Ads Instagram Ads Meta Advantage+
Recommended structure: 3 campaigns — Awareness (Reels/Video), Consideration (Before/After), Conversion (Lead Form). Run all through Meta Advantage+ for smart budget allocation.

📹 Campaign 1 — Brand Awareness

  • Objective: Video Views / Reach
  • Format: 15–30s Reels showing project transformations
  • Copy hook: "This is what your backyard could look like..."
  • Placement: Instagram Reels + Facebook Feed
  • Budget: 20% of META spend
  • Geo: 50km radius from Port Sorell

🖼️ Campaign 2 — Consideration

  • Objective: Traffic / Engagement
  • Format: Carousel with before/after photos
  • Copy: Testimonials + project stats
  • Retarget: Website visitors + video viewers 50%+
  • Budget: 30% of META spend
  • CTA: "See our work" → Portfolio page

📋 Campaign 3 — Lead Gen

  • Objective: Leads (native META lead form)
  • Format: Single image or short video
  • Offer: "Free quote — we come to you"
  • Form fields: Name, suburb, type of work, best time to call
  • Budget: 50% of META spend
  • Warm audiences only (retargeting)

⚡ Meta Advantage+ Tips

  • Enable Advantage+ Creative to auto-test headlines/images
  • Use Advantage+ Audience for prospecting campaigns
  • Set up Conversions API alongside Pixel for iOS data loss
  • Enable Click-to-WhatsApp as a lead form alternative
  • A/B test: lead form vs landing page destination

🛠️ How to install the META Pixel and launch your first campaign — step by step
▼ Show steps
1
Create a Meta Business Suite account
Go to business.facebook.com and sign in with your personal Facebook. Click "Create Account," enter the business name "Mint Exterior," your name, and business email. This is separate from your personal Facebook and controls all your ads.
Create Business Suite account →
2
Create a Facebook Pixel
In Business Suite, go to Events Manager (left menu) → Connect Data Sources → Web → Meta Pixel. Name it "Mint Exterior Pixel" and enter your website URL. Click "Continue."
3
Install the Pixel on your website
META will show you a pixel code. If your site is on Squarespace: go to Settings → Advanced → Code Injection → paste the pixel code in the Header section. If on Wix or Wordpress there are dedicated META Pixel plugins. Click "Send Test Traffic" to confirm it's working.
4
Create your first ad campaign
Go to Ads Manager → Create. Choose "Awareness" as the campaign objective. Name it "Mint Exterior — Awareness — [Month]". Turn on Advantage Campaign Budget and set $10/day. Click Next.
5
Set your audience in the Ad Set
Location: Tasmania + surrounding areas (50km from Port Sorell). Age: 30–65. Under "Detailed Targeting" add: Home improvement, Landscaping, Decking, Home renovation, Homeowner. Turn on Advantage+ Audience.
6
Upload your creative and launch
At the Ad level, upload your best before/after Reel or photo. Write your headline ("Tassie's backyard specialists") and body copy from the formulas above. Set the CTA to "Learn More" pointing to your website. Click Publish and wait for META to approve (usually 24hrs).
Important: Do not change budgets or audiences for the first 7 days. META needs time to learn. You'll see "Learning Phase" in the campaign status — this is normal. Only pause if you're spending money with zero results after 14 days.

Google Ads Campaign Structure
Search LSA Performance Max
Priority #1: Local Services Ads (LSA) — these appear above all other results and are pay-per-lead, not pay-per-click. Perfect for trades. Set these up first, everything else is secondary.

⭐ Priority 1 — Local Services Ads (LSA)

  • Shows "Google Guaranteed" badge — massive trust signal
  • Pay per lead, not per click (huge cost efficiency)
  • Appears above standard ads for "decking near me" etc
  • Requires licence verification (standard trades requirement)
  • Categories: Fencing, Landscaping, Carpentry, Concrete

🔎 Search Campaign — High Intent

  • Exact/phrase match keywords only
  • Keywords: "decking Launceston", "fence builder Tasmania", "concrete paving Devonport"
  • Negative keywords: DIY, how to, free, cheap, Sydney/Melbourne
  • Enable call extensions + location extensions
  • Smart Bidding: Maximize Conversions, then shift to tCPA

🗺️ Google Business Profile (free)

  • Fully optimised GBP is essential before any ads
  • Add photos weekly from completed jobs
  • Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
  • Enable messaging and booking features
  • Post updates fortnightly

🚀 Performance Max (Phase 2)

  • Launch after Search has at least 30 conversions/month
  • Feed it: all photos, videos, headlines, descriptions
  • Exclude brand terms (let Search handle those)
  • Use audience signals: website visitors, past customers
  • Review search terms report weekly to add negatives

🛠️ How to set up Google LSA and Google Search campaigns — step by step
▼ Show steps
1
Set up Google Business Profile first
Go to business.google.com and sign in with a Google account. Search for "Mint Exterior" — if it already exists, click "Claim this business." If not, click "Add your business." Add your business category as "Outdoor Furniture Store" and "General Contractor." Verify ownership by postcard or phone.
Set up Google Business Profile →
2
Fully optimise your Business Profile
Upload at least 10 project photos. Write a full business description (mention decking, fencing, concrete, cladding, Northern Tasmania, Port Sorell). Add your service areas. Set business hours. Enable the "Request a Quote" messaging button. This is free and drives leads before you even run an ad.
3
Apply for Local Services Ads (LSA)
Go to ads.google.com/local-services-ads. Click "Get Started." Search for your business category (try "Fencing," "Decking," "General Contractor"). Follow the application — you'll need to verify your business licence, insurance, and identity. This process takes 1–3 weeks. Start it immediately as it has the best ROI of all channels.
Apply for Google LSA →
4
Create a Google Ads account
Go to ads.google.com and sign in with your Google account. Click "New campaign." When asked for your advertising goal, choose "Get more website traffic." Enter your website URL. When Google tries to set up a Smart Campaign, scroll to the bottom and click "Switch to Expert Mode" — this gives you full control.
Open Google Ads →
5
Create your first Search campaign
In Expert Mode, choose "Search" as the campaign type. Goal: Website visits. Uncheck Search Network Partners (keep it Google only). Set location to Tasmania + 50km around Port Sorell. Set daily budget to $20/day to start. Bidding: choose "Clicks" to begin (switch to Maximize Conversions after 30 days).
6
Build your Ad Groups and Keywords
Create one ad group per service: Decking, Fencing, Concrete, Cladding, Backyard. In each ad group, add 5–8 phrase-match keywords (wrap in quotes): "deck builders Launceston", "timber decking Tasmania", etc. Add negatives at campaign level: [DIY], [how to], [free], [cheap], [Melbourne], [Sydney].
7
Write your responsive search ads
Each ad group needs one responsive search ad. Add 10–15 headlines (e.g. "Decking Experts — Northern Tasmania", "Free Quote, Fast Response", "20+ Years Experience in TAS"). Add 4 descriptions. Google will automatically mix and match to find what works best. Add call extensions with your phone number and location extensions linking to your GBP.
8
Set up conversion tracking
Go to Tools → Conversions → New Conversion Action → Website. If you have a "Thank You" page after form submissions, use the URL. Otherwise use Google Tag Manager to track button clicks on your quote form. This tells Google which clicks turned into leads so it can optimise automatically.
Budget tip: Start your Search campaign at $20/day ($600/mo). Don't touch it for 3 weeks. After that, check your Search Terms report weekly (Keywords → Search Terms) and add irrelevant terms as negatives. This is the most important weekly task for Google Ads.

  • Summer (Oct–Feb): boost deck/outdoor budgets 30%
  • Winter (May–Jul): push cladding/fencing

🛠️ How to set daily budgets and dayparting in META and Google — step by step
▼ Show steps
1
Set META campaign daily budgets
In Ads Manager, when creating a campaign, enable "Advantage Campaign Budget" at the campaign level. Set the daily budget here rather than per ad set — META will automatically shift budget to whichever ad set is performing best. Start: Awareness $7/day, Consideration $10/day, Lead Gen $15/day.
2
Set META spend alerts
In Ads Manager → Tools → Automated Rules → Create Rule. Set: "If campaign spend exceeds $[monthly limit] in the last 30 days → Send notification + Pause campaign." This prevents overspend if you forget to check.
3
Set META ad scheduling (dayparting)
When creating an ad set, under Budget & Schedule, select "Run ads on a schedule." Untick overnight hours (10pm to 6am) and Sundays if leads don't typically convert then. Note: this option is only available if you choose "Lifetime Budget" instead of daily budget — set a monthly lump sum instead.
4
Set Google Ads daily budget
In your Google Search campaign, go to Settings → Budget. Set to $20/day ($600/mo). Google may overspend on high-traffic days but will even out over the month. For LSA, go to your LSA dashboard → Budget → set a weekly budget of $100–$150.
5
Set Google Ad Schedule
In Google Ads → your campaign → Ad Schedule. Click the pencil icon and set hours to Mon–Sat 6am–8pm only. Remove Sunday and overnight. This focuses your budget on hours when homeowners are actively searching and you can actually answer calls.
6
The 20% scaling rule
After month 2, if a campaign's cost-per-lead is under $150 and leads are converting to booked quotes, increase that campaign's budget by 20% (not more). Increase once per week maximum. Sudden large increases reset the learning algorithm and hurt performance.

Create a free Canva account and brand kit
Go to canva.com and sign up free. In the left sidebar, click "Brand Kit." Upload your logo, set your brand colours (use the hex codes from your website or existing materials), and choose your brand font. Every time you create an ad in Canva, your brand will auto-apply.
Open Canva →
2
Create your 3 reusable ad templates in Canva
Create New → Facebook Ad (1200x628px). Template 1: Before/After — split screen with project photos, "Before" and "After" labels, logo at top right, "Free Quote" button graphic at bottom. Template 2: Testimonial — dark background, quote in large text, customer name, project type. Template 3: Offer — "Get a Free Quote This Week" with a project photo background. Download CapCut and edit your first before/after Reel
Download CapCut (free, iPhone or Android). Open it → New Project → select your "before" video/photos and "after" video/photos. Tap + to add them in sequence. Drag the timeline to trim each clip to 3–5 seconds. Tap "Text" to add "BEFORE" and "AFTER" labels. Tap "Audio" to add trending music (CapCut shows trending tracks).
Download CapCut →
4
Add captions automatically
In CapCut, tap "Text" → "Auto Captions." CapCut will transcribe any narration you did on the video automatically. Edit any mistakes, then style the captions (bold, white with black outline works best for Reels). This massively increases watch time because most people watch with sound off.
5
Export and schedule in Meta Business Suite
In CapCut, tap Export → 1080p. Save to your camera roll. Open Meta Business Suite (free app or web) → Create Post → Reel. Upload the video. Write a short caption (2–3 lines max, end with "Link in bio for a free quote"). Use the schedule feature to queue it for Tuesday or Wednesday 8–10am — highest engagement times for trades content in Australia.
