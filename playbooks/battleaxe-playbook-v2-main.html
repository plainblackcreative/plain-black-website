Ditch the $4K Booking System
Square Appointments Free WaiverForever

You're paying ~$4K/year for a booking system. That's $333/month for something free tools handle just as well for a venue at your volume. The switch takes one afternoon. The ROI is immediate.

🏆 Best Free Replacement

  • Square Appointments — free tier, takes payment at booking, sends reminders, group capacity settings
  • Acuity Scheduling — $16/mo NZD if you want intake forms and waiver data built into the flow
  • Calendly + Stripe — $0/month nuclear option. Calendly handles scheduling, Stripe handles payment. Simple.

📋 What Your Booking Page Must Include

  • Session types: Walk-in (1hr), Group (2hrs), Corporate (2-3hrs), League
  • Pricing shown upfront: $40/person, 11+ contact for rates
  • Waiver acceptance before booking confirms — saves time on arrival
  • Auto reminder email 24hr before, SMS 2hr before
  • Max 4 per lane enforced — no overbooking
⚠ Do This First Before cancelling your current system: export every customer email address. This is your remarketing list. It is worth real money. Do not skip this step.

🛠️ How to install the META Pixel and launch your first campaign — step by step

2
Install the Pixel

Create Campaign 1 (Weekend Fill)

4
Set your audience
Location: Christchurch + 30km radius. Age: 22-45. Interests: axe throwing, indoor activities, team building, corporate events. Exclude people who've already visited your booking page (website custom audience).

Upload your creative
Use a 15-second vertical video (9:16) of an axe sticking in slow-mo + group celebrating. Headline: "Christchurch's Biggest Axe Throwing Venue" — Description: "10 lanes, digital targets, from $40/person." CTA: Book Now → your Square booking URL.
6
Publish and wait
Publish the campaign. Do NOT touch it for 7 days — META needs 50 events to exit the "learning phase." After day 7: if cost-per-click is above $3 NZD, test a new creative. If below $2, increase budget 20%.

Content That Sells Itself
CapCut Canva Instagram Reels TikTok

Axe throwing is one of the most visual, shareable activities in existence. A slow-motion clip of an axe sticking clean in the bullseye beats any paid ad. Your content strategy is simple: film everything, post consistently, and make it effortless for customers to do your marketing for you.

🎬 Content That Works

  • The Stick Reel: slo-mo axe landing in bullseye. 5-8 seconds + trending audio. Post weekly.
  • The Reaction: first-timer's face when their axe sticks. Genuine emotion = shares.
  • The Group Win: hens/corporate group celebrating. Tag organiser — they share to entire group.
  • The Digital Game: show your 4 digital projection lanes. Most people don't know you have these — it's your differentiator.
  • Skills Progress: "First throw vs 1 hour later." High retention, high shares.

📅 Posting Schedule (30 min/week)

  • Instagram + Facebook: 3-4 posts/week, mix of Reels and static
  • TikTok: 2-3 posts/week (reuse Reels, remove watermark via SnapTik)
  • Google Business Profile: 1 post/week (photo or promotion)
  • Best times: Thu 6-8pm, Fri 12-2pm + 6-8pm, Sat 10am-12pm
  • Caption always includes: booking link + "Christchurch axe throwing" + hashtags
Make Customers Do Your Marketing
