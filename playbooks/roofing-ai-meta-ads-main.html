Targeting Guide Setup

Recommended audience configurations for each campaign stage. These are starting points — test and narrow based on CPL and CPC after the first 7 days.

Cold — Interest Based
  • Job title: Roofing contractor, Roofer, Trades business owner
  • Interests: Small business, Roofing, Home improvement, Trades
  • Age: 28–55
  • Exclude: Employees (not business owners)
  • Geography: Australia, NZ, USA, Canada — by state/region
  • Budget: $10–20/day per ad set to start
Cold — Lookalike
  • 1% lookalike of landing page visitors (need 200+ for quality)
  • 1% lookalike of email list once you have 500+ contacts
  • 1% lookalike of 95% Reel viewers
  • Broaden to 2–3% if CPL is too high on 1%
  • Stack with broad interest layer to keep quality up
Warm — Retargeting
  • Landing page visitors — last 30 days
  • 50%+ Reel viewers — last 14 days
  • Instagram profile engagers — last 60 days
  • Demo page visitors who didn't convert
  • Use direct offer ads for this audience
  • Budget: $5–10/day — smaller audience, higher intent
Creatives to Test First
  • Reel: "9pm lead" script vs "estimate demo" script
  • Feed: Pain hook vs curiosity hook — split test week 1
  • Story: List card vs Before/After card
  • Headline A: "Stop losing leads overnight" vs "Free to set up"
  • Winner after 1,000 impressions each — kill the loser
Headline and CTA Options
  • "See the free sample" — for cold audiences
  • "Get your free 2 sections" — more specific
  • "$97. Personalised to your business." — for retargeting
  • "How roofers save 14 hrs/week" — curiosity frame
  • Button: Learn More (cold), Get Quote (warm), Shop Now (hot)
Campaign Structure
  • Campaign 1: Awareness — Reel views, broad cold audience
  • Campaign 2: Traffic — Feed ads to landing page, interest audiences
  • Campaign 3: Leads — Lead form ads, warm retargeting
  • Campaign 4: Conversions — Retargeting visitors who didn't buy
  • Run all four simultaneously once landing page is live
