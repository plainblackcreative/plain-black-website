The problem

Three traps most local businesses are stuck in.

If any of these sound familiar, this playbook was built for you.

😶

Ignoring reviews entirely

Reviews sit unanswered for weeks. Every potential customer who reads them sees a business that doesn't care. Your competitors who respond are taking your customers.

🤖

Copy-pasting generic responses

"Thank you for your kind feedback!" doesn't fool anyone. Customers can tell when a response is a template. It signals that you're not actually paying attention.

😤

Getting the negative ones wrong

One defensive or dismissive reply to a 1-star review can do more damage than the review itself. Most business owners don't know what to say, so they say nothing.

Fake and malicious reviews you don't know how to fight

Competitors leave fake 1-stars. Disgruntled ex-employees pile on. You don't know what you can do about it, so you do nothing.

Section 04: AI Advisor
You →
A customer left a 1-star review saying the wait was too long and the staff were rude. What's the best way to respond right now?
AI →
Respond within 24 hours. Acknowledge specifically what they said. Don't be vague. "Long wait and rude staff" are two separate issues, so address both briefly. Apologise without excuses. Offer to make it right offline. Keep it under 100 words. Don't ask them to come back in the response; that reads as desperate. The goal is to show future readers that you take feedback seriously. That's who you're really writing for.
