TRAINING GAMES

WE LEARN THROUGH GAMES
Gate Passing Skill: Passing Accuracy
Player A → Gate → Player B. Focus on clean, accurate push passes through the gate.
Triangle Passing Skill: Pass & Move
A at top, B and C at base. Pass around the triangle and move to the next position.
Chaos Dribbling Skill: Ball Control & Awareness
Players dribble inside a square, avoiding each other while maintaining control.
10 Pass Challenge Skill: Team Passing
Teams complete 10 passes in a row without losing the ball. Celebrate together!

TEAM LEADERS

WE HAVE FOUR LEADERS
🏆
CAPTAIN
Encourages the team. Sets the tone on and off the field.
🛡️
DEFENSIVE LEADER
Organises the defence. Communicates shape and positioning.
⚡
MIDFIELD LEADER
Keeps the team moving. Links defence to attack.
🔥
ENERGY LEADER
Brings enthusiasm. Lifts the team when it's tough.

Leadership rotates during the season.
