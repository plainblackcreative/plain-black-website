Speed-to-lead is the job. Stop losing calls before you spend on ads.
Pending
Goal Set up a system so no inbound lead goes unanswered, day or night. The fastest responder wins. Fix this before spending a dollar on ads, or Test XX will pay for traffic it cannot capture.
This is the biggest leak Most test owners lose 30 to 50% of potential bookings to missed calls and slow callbacks. Responding within 5 minutes lifts conversion by over 300% compared to 30 minutes.
Voicemail with no callback commitmentIf the voicemail does not say "we call back within 30 minutes," callers assume they will wait forever and call a competitor.
No after-hours coveragetest emergencies happen at 10pm. Without a capture system, Test XX loses its highest-urgency, lowest-price-sensitivity jobs.
Slow quote follow-upSend a quote, wait for callback. Most do not. Follow up within 2 hours or the job is gone.
Single phone point of failureOne mobile, no backup. One busy signal loses a job. A virtual number with forwarding costs less than one missed job per year.
DIY Steps Set up missed call text-back automation
▾

  3. 3
    Set up missed call text-back via Jobber, Tradify, or ServiceM8 or SimpleTexting. Message: "Hi, this is Test XX. Sorry we missed you. What can we help with? We'll reply in minutes."
  4. 4
    Document a quote follow-up SOP: call within 2 hours of sending. Text at 24 hours if no response. Final call at 72 hours. Share with everyone who sends quotes.

Most jobs are lost in the follow-up, not the ad
Pending
Goal Build a follow-up sequence that converts every inbound lead into a booked estimate, automatically and consistently. Test should never have to manually remember to follow up on a quote.

Follow-up sequence

  • Immediate: auto-text within 2 minutes of lead
  • Hour 1: personal call from Test or dispatcher
  • Hour 24: text if no response to quote sent
  • Hour 72: final call, offer to answer questions
  • Day 7: email with any current test promotion

Text templates that work

  • "Hi, Test XX here. Got your request. Calling you now."
  • "Just sent your quote. Any questions I can answer?"
  • "We have a slot this week in test. Want me to hold it?"
  • "Following up one last time. Happy to chat whenever."
Set this up before running ads Without a follow-up system, Test XX is paying to generate leads that go cold. The average trades business follows up on fewer than 30% of quotes.
DIY Steps Set up Jobber, Tradify, or ServiceM8 for lead follow-up
▾
  1. 1
    Sign up for Jobber, Tradify, or ServiceM8. Create a pipeline with stages: New Lead, Contacted, Quote Sent, Booked, Closed. Every inbound lead from Meta and Google goes into New Lead immediately.
  2. 2
    Create an automation: when a lead enters New Lead, send the instant auto-text. Set a task for Test or dispatcher to call within 60 minutes.
  3. 3
    When a quote is sent, move the lead to Quote Sent. Automation triggers: text at 24 hours, call reminder at 72 hours. If no response after 7 days, send the final email and close the lead.
  4. 4
    Connect Meta leads directly to Jobber, Tradify, or ServiceM8 via Zapier or native integration. Every Meta form submission should appear in the pipeline within 5 minutes. Test this before running any Meta campaigns.
