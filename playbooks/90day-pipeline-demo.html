01
Google Business Profile
The free foundation that drives local calls
Free Sample
Goal A fully optimized Google Business Profile that puts you in the map pack for high-intent local searches. This is free traffic you are currently leaving on the table every single day.

What to fix first

  • Business name exactly matches your website
  • Primary category set to your specific trade type
  • Service area matches where you actually work
  • Hours correct, including emergency availability
  • At least 10 photos: trucks, team, before and after

What moves the ranking needle

  • Review velocity: 1 to 2 new reviews per week
  • Responding to every review within 24 hours
  • Weekly posts showing recent jobs in your city
  • Services list complete with your specific job types
  • Q&A section populated with your own answered questions
Common mistake Setting your service area too narrow. Add every city you can realistically reach in under 45 minutes. Google shows you to searchers based on your service area, not just your business address.
DIY Steps How to set up your service area correctly
▾
  1. 1
    Go to business.google.com and select your profile. Click "Edit profile" on the left panel.
  2. 2
    Click "Business location." If you operate service-area only with no customer-facing storefront, turn off the physical address toggle. Then click "Service area."
  3. 3
    Add your primary city plus every surrounding city you serve within 45 minutes. Do not add areas you cannot serve reliably. More is not better if you cannot realistically cover those areas.
  4. 4
    Click Save. Google may re-verify your profile. Check your email for verification steps within 48 hours.
Sanity check Search your trade type and city on Google Maps on your phone. If your business appears in the map pack, this is set up correctly. If not visible after 2 weeks with a complete profile, check your verification status at business.google.com.
  • Business name matches website exactly, no keyword stuffing in the name field
  • Primary category set to the correct trade contractor type
  • Service area covers all cities you actually serve within 45 minutes
  • Business hours correct, including after-hours emergency note if applicable
  • At least 10 photos uploaded: trucks, team, finished work
  • Services list complete with your specific job types and descriptions
  • Description written mentioning your city and main services naturally

02
Local Service Ads Setup
Get the Google Guaranteed badge and pay per lead, not per click
Free Sample
Goal Get your Google Guaranteed badge and appear in LSA results above regular search ads. LSAs charge per qualified lead, not per click, and the badge significantly increases call rates for trades businesses.

What LSAs give you

  • Google Guaranteed badge next to your name
  • Appears above regular paid search results
  • Pay per qualified lead, dispute bad ones for credits
  • Tied directly to your Google Business Profile
  • Call recording for every lead received

What you need to apply

  • Valid contractor's license for your trade and state
  • Current business insurance certificate
  • Background check for owner and employees
  • Business registration documents
  • Verified Google Business Profile (Section 01)
Plan for 1 to 3 weeks The LSA verification process takes time. Submit your documents as soon as Section 01 is complete. Do not wait until you are ready to run ads. The clock starts when you apply.
DIY Steps How to apply for Google Local Service Ads
▾
  1. 1
    Go to ads.google.com/local-services-ads. Click "Get started" and sign in with the same Google account as your Google Business Profile.
  2. 2
    Select your business category and service area. Be precise. LSA results are location-specific and your service area must match your Google Business Profile.
  3. 3
    Upload your documents: license certificate, insurance certificate, and business registration as PDFs. Have all three ready before you start. Missing documents will pause the application.
  4. 4
    Complete the background check process for yourself and any customer-facing employees. This is done through a third-party provider linked from the LSA dashboard.
  5. 5
    Set your weekly budget. Start at $200 to $400 per week depending on your trade and market. Set it too low and Google will not show your listing enough to generate leads.
Sanity check After submitting, your LSA dashboard should show "In review" status. If it shows "Action required" for more than 3 days, log in and check which document is flagged. Expired insurance certificates are the most common blocker.
  • Contractor license certificate ready as a PDF, current and not expired
  • Insurance certificate ready with correct coverage amounts
  • LSA application submitted at ads.google.com/local-services-ads
  • Background check process started for owner and relevant employees
  • Weekly budget set at $200 minimum to see meaningful lead volume
  • Application status confirmed as "In review" in LSA dashboard
