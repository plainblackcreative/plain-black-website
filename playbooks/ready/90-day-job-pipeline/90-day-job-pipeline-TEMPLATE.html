Call Handling System
Speed-to-lead is the job. Stop losing calls before you spend on ads.
Pending
Goal Set up a system so no inbound lead goes unanswered, day or night. The fastest responder wins. Fix this before spending a dollar on ads, or [[BUSINESS_NAME]] will pay for traffic it cannot capture.
This is the biggest leak Most [[TRADE_TYPE]] owners lose 30 to 50% of potential bookings to missed calls and slow callbacks. Responding within 5 minutes lifts conversion by over 300% compared to 30 minutes.
Voicemail with no callback commitmentIf the voicemail does not say "we call back within 30 minutes," callers assume they will wait forever and call a competitor.
No after-hours coverage[[TRADE_TYPE]] emergencies happen at 10pm. Without a capture system, [[BUSINESS_NAME]] loses its highest-urgency, lowest-price-sensitivity jobs.
Slow quote follow-upSend a quote, wait for callback. Most do not. Follow up within 2 hours or the job is gone.
Single phone point of failureOne mobile, no backup. One busy signal loses a job. A virtual number with forwarding costs less than one missed job per year.
DIY Steps Set up missed call text-back automation
▾
  1. 1
    Set up a Google Voice number (free) or OpenPhone (~$15/mo). Forward all calls to [[OWNER_FIRST_NAME]]'s mobile. Do not use a personal mobile as the primary business number.
  2. 2
    Update the voicemail to: "You've reached [[BUSINESS_NAME]]. We're on a job right now but we'll call you back within 30 minutes. If this is an emergency, text us at this number." Record this yourself. Do not use the default carrier voicemail.
  3. 3
    Set up missed call text-back via [[CRM_TOOL]] or SimpleTexting. Message: "Hi, this is [[BUSINESS_NAME]]. Sorry we missed you. What can we help with? We'll reply in minutes."
  4. 4
    Document a quote follow-up SOP: call within 2 hours of sending. Text at 24 hours if no response. Final call at 72 hours. Share with everyone who sends quotes.
Sanity check Call the [[BUSINESS_NAME]] business number from a different phone and let it go to voicemail. Did you hear the correct message? Did you receive an automated text within 2 minutes? If either fails, fix it before running any ads.

Pending
Goal Build a follow-up sequence that converts every inbound lead into a booked estimate, automatically and consistently. [[OWNER_FIRST_NAME]] should never have to manually remember to follow up on a quote.

Follow-up sequence

  • Immediate: auto-text within 2 minutes of lead
  • Hour 1: personal call from [[OWNER_FIRST_NAME]] or dispatcher
  • Hour 24: text if no response to quote sent
  • Hour 72: final call, offer to answer questions
  • Day 7: email with any current [[TRADE_TYPE]] promotion

Text templates that work

  • "Hi, [[BUSINESS_NAME]] here. Got your request. Calling you now."
  • "Just sent your quote. Any questions I can answer?"
  • "We have a slot this week in [[CITY]]. Want me to hold it?"
  • "Following up one last time. Happy to chat whenever."
Set this up before running ads Without a follow-up system, [[BUSINESS_NAME]] is paying to generate leads that go cold. The average trades business follows up on fewer than 30% of quotes.
DIY Steps Set up [[CRM_TOOL]] for lead follow-up
▾

    Sign up for [[CRM_TOOL]]. Create a pipeline with stages: New Lead, Contacted, Quote Sent, Booked, Closed. Every inbound lead from Meta and Google goes into New Lead immediately.

  3. 3
