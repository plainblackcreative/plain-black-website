Why this matters now

If your review profile looks like most small business owners', you're losing customers every week.

Your Google profile is the first thing most new customers see. More than your website. More than your socials. Here's what's costing you money right now.

✕

You're ignoring your reviews

Unanswered reviews tell every future customer that nobody's paying attention. That signal alone sends them to the competitor next door who looks engaged.

✕

Your responses sound copy-pasted

"Thank you for your feedback, we value your business." Every future customer can tell that's a canned response. Generic replies are worse than no reply at all.

✕

One bad review is tanking your rating

Without a steady stream of new 5-stars, a single 1-star review drags your rating down for months. Most owners have no system for consistently generating positive reviews.

What a real response looks like

Generic responses are worse than no response. Personal ones turn sceptics into customers.

Here's the difference between what most owners write and what the playbook shows you to write. Same review. Two very different outcomes for every future customer who reads it.

✕ Generic response
"Waited over an hour. Nobody kept us updated. Won't be back."
"Dear valued customer, we're sorry to hear about your negative experience. Please contact us at our main email so we can further assist you. We take all feedback seriously and appreciate the opportunity to improve. Thank you for your patience."
✓ AI-drafted, edited to sound like you
"Waited over an hour. Nobody kept us updated. Won't be back."
"Waiting that long without an update is on us, regardless of how busy we were. That's not okay. Please reach out directly and we'll sort it properly. We'd genuinely like the chance to do better by you."
