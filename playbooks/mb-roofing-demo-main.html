AI Lead Follow-Up System
Every M&B Roofing enquiry gets an instant reply, whether Michael is on a roof or off the clock
Make.com — Free Gmail — Free

M&B Roofing gets enquiries through mbroofinghobart.com.au and HiPages. Most come in outside business hours. A Hobart homeowner notices a leak on a wet winter evening, submits the form, and the first roofer who replies gets the job. This section builds an instant reply system using Make.com and Gmail. Costs nothing. Takes under an hour to set up.

The Goal

Every enquiry to mbroofinghobart.com.au gets an instant, personal reply whether Michael is on a roof in Moonah or finishing up in Sorell. The message names the service they asked about and offers a free measure and quote. Cost: $0.

What to automate for M&B Roofing
  • SMS within 90 seconds of any form submission
  • Missed-call text-back (instant SMS if you don't answer)
  • After-hours email confirmation sequence
  • 5-touch follow-up over 7 days for cold leads
  • Internal team notification with full lead details
Free tools that do this
  • Make.com — free tier (1,000 ops/month)
  • Gmail — automated replies from your inbox
  • Calendly free — booking link in follow-up email
  • Google Sheets — auto-log every lead
  • Tally.so — free contact form if needed
Common Mistake

Do not send generic follow-up messages. "Hi, thanks for your inquiry" does not convert. Your AI should reference the specific request — storm damage, full reroof, inspection — and ask a qualifying question that starts a conversation.

  1. 1Go to make.com and create a free account. No credit card required.
  2. 2Click "Create a new scenario." Add a trigger module for your contact form. Make.com connects to Google Forms, Tally, Typeform, and most website builders. Select "Watch new responses."
  3. 3Add a Gmail action: "Send an email." Connect michael@mbroofinghobart.com.au (or your business Gmail). Write your reply — under 80 words. Name the service they asked about. Ask one question: "Are you looking to get this sorted in the next few weeks?" Include your free measure and quote Calendly link.
  4. 4Add a second Gmail action for internal notification: sends Michael the lead name, suburb, service requested, email, and phone. Subject: "New M&B Roofing Enquiry from [suburb]." Fires every time a new enquiry hits the site.
  5. 5Add a Google Sheets action: "Add a row." Log every lead automatically so nothing gets lost.
  6. 6Click "Run once" to test. Submit a fake enquiry through your contact form. Confirm the follow-up email arrives within 60 seconds.
  7. 7Turn the scenario on. Every new enquiry to mbroofinghobart.com.au now gets an instant reply whether it arrives at 9pm on a Sunday or mid-job in Murdunna. Cost: $0.
  8. 8Shortcut: Want SMS replies and missed-call text-back? GoHighLevel ($97 AUD/month) does all of this in one platform. Worth considering once M&B Roofing is handling 30+ enquiries per month.
