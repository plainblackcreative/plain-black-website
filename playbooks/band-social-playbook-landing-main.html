The problem

Great music. Basically invisible online.

Most gigging bands are brilliant live and almost impossible to find everywhere else. Promoters Google you before they call. Venues book acts that look active. New fans share what they can actually find. If your digital presence is an afterthought, your calendar will be too.

📱

Facebook-only strategy

Posting occasionally on Facebook reaches about 60 people, half of whom are already at your gigs. It's not a strategy — it's a habit.

🎬

No short-form video

Instagram Reels and TikTok are where new fans find live music now. A 30-second soundcheck clip can reach tens of thousands. Most bands never make one.

🔍

Invisible on Google

When a venue manager searches "live band [your city]," you need to appear. Google Business Profile takes 90 minutes to set up. Most bands never bother.

💼

Missing paid bookings

Corporate events, weddings, and festivals all come through digital discovery. A proper online presence turns cold enquiries from rare to regular.

🔄

No repeatable system

Posting when you remember, sharing the occasional photo, boosting something at random. That's noise, not a strategy. You need a system.

💸

Agencies cost too much

A social media manager for a band costs $500–2,000/month. For a few posts and a monthly report. This playbook replaces that for a one-off $97.
