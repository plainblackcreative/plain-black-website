Strategy Overview
Referral-First Google Premium Positioning

Section 03
Referral Systems & Reputation
Referrals Zero Cost Highest ROI
This is the #1 channel for The Marshall Method. A practice of this calibre is built on results and word of mouth. The question isn't whether William gets referrals — it's whether there's a system that makes referrals consistent, trackable, and self-amplifying.

🔁 Current Client Referrals

  • The most powerful and lowest-cost acquisition channel
  • Ask every satisfied client directly: "Who do you know who's been struggling with [condition]?"
  • Create a referral card to give to clients post-session: simple, tactile, memorable
  • Consider a "first session free" offer for referred new clients — de-risks the leap
  • A simple text from a happy client is worth 100 Facebook ads

🏃 Sports Club Partnerships

  • Approach NW Tasmania football clubs, running clubs, triathlon clubs, swimming squads
  • Offer a discounted "assessment session" for the club committee to experience the method first-hand
  • Once coaches feel the difference, referrals to injured players become automatic
  • Target clubs in: Ulverstone, Devonport, Burnie, Launceston (will travel)
  • Ask to be listed on the club's injury management resources page

🩺 GP & Specialist Referrals

  • Build relationships with local GPs who are frustrated by patients cycling through standard physio
  • Send a short personal letter + one-pager to all GP practices in Ulverstone, Devonport, Burnie
  • Focus on: post-surgical rehab, chronic sciatica, work injury — conditions where GPs see standard care fail
  • One GP who refers 2–3 patients per month transforms the practice

⭐ Google Reviews System

  • Google reviews are the #1 trust signal for new patients searching online
  • Every client who has a great result should be asked for a review before they leave
  • Send the Google review link by SMS within 24 hours of their session
  • Target: 50 reviews at 5.0 stars — this makes The Marshall Method dominate local search results
  • Respond personally to every review (builds trust with prospective clients reading them)

HBOT deserves its own dedicated marketing strategy, separate from the injury therapy practice.

  • Chronic fatigue syndrome, fibromyalgia

🛠️ How to set up the Messenger auto-reply and build a Looker Studio dashboard — step by step

1
Set up Facebook Messenger instant reply
In Meta Business Suite → Inbox → Automations → Instant Reply. Toggle on. Message: "Thank you for your enquiry. William is usually with a patient but will respond as soon as possible. To book directly, visit themarshallmethod.com.au or call 0417 778 219. If your matter is urgent, please call directly." This handles after-hours enquiries without William needing to respond immediately.
2
Build the Looker Studio dashboard
Go to lookerstudio.google.com → sign in → Create → Report. Add data sources: Google Ads (connect your account) and Meta Ads (connect via Meta Ads connector). Add: a date-range line chart for website clicks over time, a scorecard for total Google Ads spend, a table showing each campaign with spend and clicks, and a META scorecard for impressions and link clicks. Bookmark this and check it Monday mornings. 15 minutes per week — that's your entire reporting workflow.

Set up Square's confirmation email template
In your Square Appointments dashboard → Communications → Confirmation Emails. Customise the confirmation email that goes to every new booking: add a personal note from William welcoming them, what to bring, where to park, and what to expect in a 90-minute session. This reduces no-shows and anxiety for first-time patients — both important for this client type.
4
Create the Google review request SMS template
Get your Google review link: open your GBP dashboard → "Get more reviews" → copy the link. Save this contact on your phone as "Google Review Link" so you can copy it easily. Draft and save this SMS template (in your notes app): "Thanks for your session today — it was great working with you. If you'd be willing to share your experience, a Google review genuinely helps others in pain find me: [LINK]. Much appreciated — William." After every good session outcome: open template, paste link, change name, send. 45 seconds.
