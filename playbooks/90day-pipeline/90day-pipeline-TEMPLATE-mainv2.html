Call Handling System
Speed-to-lead is the job. Stop losing calls.
Pending
🎯
Goal: Set up a system so no inbound lead goes unanswered, day or night. The fastest responder wins. Fix this before spending a dollar on ads, or [[BUSINESS_NAME]] will pay for traffic it cannot capture.
🚨
This is the biggest leak. Most [[TRADE_TYPE]] owners lose 30 to 50% of potential bookings to missed calls and slow callbacks. Responding within 5 minutes increases conversion by over 300% versus 30 minutes.

Common mistakes to fix right now

Voicemail with no callback commitment If the voicemail does not say "we call back within 30 minutes," callers assume they will wait indefinitely and call a competitor.
No after-hours coverage [[TRADE_TYPE]] emergencies happen at 10pm. Without a system to capture those calls, [[BUSINESS_NAME]] is losing its highest-urgency, lowest-price-sensitivity jobs.
Slow quote follow-up Send a quote and wait for them to call back. Most do not. Follow up within 2 hours or the job is probably gone.
Single phone point of failure One mobile number, no backup. One busy signal loses a job. A virtual number with call forwarding costs less than one missed job per year.
DIY StepsSetting up missed call text-back automation
▼
1
Set up a Google Voice number (free) or a virtual business number through Grasshopper or OpenPhone (~$15/mo). Forward all calls to [[OWNER_NAME]]'s mobile. Do not use a personal mobile as the primary business number.
2
Update the voicemail message: "You've reached [[BUSINESS_NAME]]. We're on a job right now but we'll call you back within 30 minutes. If this is an emergency, text us at this number." Record this. Do not use the default carrier voicemail.
3
Set up missed call text-back. Options: Jobber (built in), HighLevel (most powerful), or Missed Call Text Back by SimpleTexting. The message: "Hi, this is [[BUSINESS_NAME]]. Sorry we missed you. What can we help you with? We'll reply in minutes."
4
Create a quote follow-up SOP: Call within 2 hours of sending. Text at 24 hours if no response. Final call at 72 hours. After that, stop. Document this and make sure every person who sends quotes follows it.
Sanity check: Call the [[BUSINESS_NAME]] business number from a different phone and let it go to voicemail. Did you hear the correct voicemail message? Did you receive an automated text within 2 minutes? If either fails, go back and fix it before running any ads.

Your 90-Day Action Schedule
Week by week. What to do and when to check it.
Pending
🎯
Goal: Follow this schedule in order. Do not skip ahead. Each week builds on the previous one. The system does not work if sections are skipped or run out of sequence.
Weeks 1 to 2 — Foundation

Complete Google Business Profile (Section 01). Submit LSA verification documents (Section 02). Set up virtual phone number and missed call text-back (Section 03). Install and verify Meta Pixel (Section 04). Start Day 1 Meta awareness campaign at [[META_BUDGET_TEST]] using interest-based targeting. Do not touch any campaigns after launch.

Weeks 3 to 4 — Capture

LSA should be verified by now. Turn on LSA ads at [[AD_BUDGET_WEEKLY]] budget. Launch Meta lead campaign at [[META_BUDGET_DAY]]. Check Google Business Profile insights: calls, direction requests, and website visits. If LSA is still in review, follow up with Google support chat and confirm no documents are flagged.

Weeks 5 to 8 — Optimize

Review Meta campaigns. Keep the top-performing ad, pause the others. Check Meta cost per lead: target under $30. Launch Google Search Ads (Section 06) if LSA alone is not generating enough volume. Send review request texts to all completed jobs from the last 30 days that have not yet left a review.

Weeks 9 to 12 — Scale

If cost per lead is under $35 and jobs are closing, increase Meta daily budget by 20%. Check if the Meta pixel audience has hit 1,000 people. If yes, activate retargeting (Section 09). At day 90: review full numbers. Leads in, calls answered, jobs booked, cost per booked job. Decide what to expand, what to cut, and what to keep.

The most common failure point: Completing setup and then not checking numbers for 6 weeks. Set a recurring 30-minute block every Monday to review: leads in, calls answered, jobs booked. One number matters: cost per booked job. Everything else is context.
