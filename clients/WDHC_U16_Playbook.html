INSIDE LEVERAGE

THE SIDELINE IS YOUR TEAMMATE

When defending near the sideline, use it. The line does your job for you on one side.

SIDELINE
ATK

Tap the attacker to see the principle in action
1
Get inside the attacker Position yourself between the attacker and the centre of the field — not between them and the sideline.
2
Force them towards the line Your body angle pushes the attacker sideways. The sideline has nowhere to go. Let the line do the work.
3
Stay patient — don't lunge Hold your shape. If you dive in, they spin back inside. Stay between them and goal until they step out.
4
Win the ball or win the line Either they lose the ball trying to beat you, or they step out. Both are a win for your team.
WRONG
Defender gets outside the attacker — now the attacker can cut inside and attack the goal with space.
RIGHT
Defender gets inside the attacker — the sideline traps them. Two defenders for the price of one.
You + the sideline = two defenders.

TRAINING GAMES

WE LEARN THROUGH GAMES
Gate Passing Skill: Passing Accuracy
Player A → Gate → Player B. Focus on clean, accurate push passes through the gate.
Triangle Passing Skill: Pass & Move
A at top, B and C at base. Pass around the triangle and move to the next position.
Chaos Dribbling Skill: Ball Control & Awareness
Players dribble inside a square, avoiding each other while maintaining control.
10 Pass Challenge Skill: Team Passing
Teams complete 10 passes in a row without losing the ball. Celebrate together!
